Chicken and Rice

The dish typically consists of flavorful, marinated and grilled or roasted chicken served over a bed of aromatic seasoned rice. It's often accompanied by vegetables, usually lettuce, tomatoes, and onions, and is generously drizzled with sauces, such as tahini, yogurt-based sauces, or hot sauces.

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken Breasts
  • 2 cups water
  • 1(10.5 ounce) can of cream of chicken soup
  • 1 can cream of celery soup
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • salt and ground black peeper to taste
  • 1/2 cup butter. sliced into pats

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the over to 400 degrees F. Grease sides and bottom of casserole dish.
  2. Stir chicken, water, rice, cream of chicken soup, cream of celery soup, and cream of mushroom soup together in the prepared casserole dish; season with salt and pepper.
  3. Arrange butter evenly over the top of the chicken mixture.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven unti the rice is tender and the chicken is cooked through, 1 hour to 75 minutes.
  5. Cool 10-15 minutes before serving. Enjoy!!
