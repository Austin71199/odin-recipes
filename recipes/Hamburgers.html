Hamburgers

Description

A hamburger is a sandwich made of a cooked ground meat patty in a sliced bun or roll, often served with toppings and condiments.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • bacon
  • ketchup
  • Hamburger Buns
  1. Divide the ground beef into 4 or 6 portions, depending on the number of burgers you would like to make.
  2. Shape the patties into a round, circular shape.
  3. Warm the pan and if prefferred add cooking oil.
  4. Toast the buns on another pan until slightly golden-brown.
  5. Increase the heat to medium-high and add the burgers to the skillet.
  6. Cook the burgers to desired doneness.
  7. Flip the burgers
  8. Add the condiments and toppings as you desire.
  9. To make cheeseburgers, add cheese to the top of the burgers.
